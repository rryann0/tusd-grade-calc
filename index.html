About

This tool helps students predict their GPA, track graduation requirements, and calculate final grades.

Calculator Info

  • Regular classes are based on a 4.0 scale. (A = 4.0, B = 3.0, etc.)
  • Honors and AP classes are based on a 5.0 scale. (A = 5.0, B = 4.0, etc.)
  • Your "points" for a class are your scale number (5.0, 4.0, etc.) multiplied by the credit hours you received (10 for 1 semester).
  • Your weighted GPA is then calculated by averaging the sum of these "points" with your credit hours.

Additional Info

  • Dual enrollment classes outside of partnership classes (like history classes) are classified as regular classes.
  • PE/Sports classes are not counted towards your weighted GPA.
